<p><p>The inverter charger market comprises three main types: pure sine wave, modified sine wave, and split phase. Pure sine wave inverters provide clean, stable power suitable for sensitive electronics and appliances. Modified sine wave inverters offer a cost-effective solution for less sensitive devices but may cause inefficiencies in some electronics. Split phase inverters are designed for 240V systems, commonly used in North America, allowing the powering of larger appliances. Each type serves specific needs within various applications and consumer preferences.</p></p>
